About Teslamattress

Our Mission

Teslamattress is an independent review site dedicated to helping Tesla owners find the best camping mattresses for their vehicles. We purchase, test, and review every mattress ourselves, spending real nights sleeping in our Teslas to provide honest, first-hand assessments.

Unlike manufacturer-sponsored reviews, we have no obligation to any brand. Our ratings reflect our genuine experience, and we update reviews as products evolve.

Our Testing Process

Every mattress we review goes through a comprehensive testing protocol:

  • Multi-night testing: Minimum 5 nights of actual use before publishing
  • Real conditions: Testing in various temperatures, locations, and scenarios
  • Long-term evaluation: Updates after 3+ months of use
  • Comparison testing: Side-by-side comparisons when possible
  • User feedback integration: We monitor real owner reviews on Trustpilot, Reddit, and forums

Our scoring system evaluates five key areas: Comfort, Build Quality, Fit & Finish, Ease of Setup, and Value for Money.

How We're Funded

Teslamattress earns money through affiliate partnerships. When you use our discount codes or click our links to purchase a mattress, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you.

Important: Affiliate relationships never influence our ratings. We've given low scores to products from brands we partner with, and high scores to brands without affiliate programs. Our credibility depends on honest reviews.

Who's Behind Teslamattress?

I'm Fredrik—a developer by day, Tesla camper by obsession. In 2017, I bought a Model S and immediately wanted to road trip in it. The problem? Finding a mattress that didn't feel like sleeping on a pool float.

Five returns and a lot of back pain later, I started documenting what worked and what didn't. That documentation became this site.

These days I test mattresses with my family on trips along the Norwegian coast. My wife has opinions about firmness. The kids just want to watch movies in Camp Mode.

I use "we" throughout the site because my family helps with testing, and because it's not just about me—it's about helping you find the right mattress.

To date, we've:

  • Tested 10+ different Tesla mattresses
  • Spent 100+ nights sleeping in our Teslas
  • Driven 50,000+ km on camping road trips
  • Helped thousands of Tesla owners find the right mattress
