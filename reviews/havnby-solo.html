Why Choose the Solo Edition?

The Havnby Autolevel Solo isn't just a smaller mattress - it's a different approach to Tesla camping. Here's when it makes sense:

Gear-Heavy Camping

The Solo leaves approximately 20 inches of space alongside the mattress. That's enough for a cooler, camera equipment, hiking gear, or anything else you need accessible overnight.

Traveling with Pets

Many solo campers travel with dogs. The Solo gives your furry companion their own space in the car without sacrificing your sleep comfort.

Frequent Campers

At just 7 lbs, the Solo is the lightest premium Tesla mattress available. If you're setting up and breaking down camp frequently, this weight savings adds up.

Mixed-Use Road Trips

On trips where you're alternating between hotels and car camping, the Solo's compact size means you're not constantly rearranging your entire cargo area.

Zero-Tilt Technology Explained

When you fold down your Tesla's rear seats, they don't create a flat surface - there's a noticeable angle. Havnby's zero-tilt design uses strategically placed foam chambers to compensate for this angle, creating a sleeping surface that's actually level.

This might sound like marketing speak, but it makes a real difference. Without zero-tilt correction, you spend the night slowly sliding toward the front seats. The Havnby keeps you in place all night.

Setup Guide: Ready to Sleep in Under 2 Minutes

The Havnby Solo's built-in pump makes setup fast and hassle-free.

  1. Fold down the rear seats - The Model Y's seats fold nearly flat. You can leave one side up if you want a backseat passenger.
  2. Remove from the compact storage bag - At only 7 lbs, the Solo is easy to grab from your trunk.
  3. Position against one side - Place the mattress along either the left or right side of the cargo area, leaving space for gear.
  4. Press the inflate button - The built-in pump inflates to optimal firmness in about 50 seconds. The dual pump system is designed for durability.
  5. Arrange your gear - Use the 20 inches of side space for a cooler, backpack, camera bag, or pet bed.
  6. Enable Camp Mode - Keep your Tesla's climate control running all night.

Solo Camping Pro Tips

  • Keep a small flashlight accessible in the side space for nighttime convenience
  • Position your pillow at the back of the car for more legroom toward the front seats
  • The side space works great for wet gear you don't want on the mattress
  • Consider a window shade kit for privacy while solo camping

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you fit gear alongside the Havnby Solo mattress?

Yes, that's the main advantage of the Solo edition. The mattress is 32 inches wide, leaving significant space for a cooler, camping gear, luggage, or even a pet. Many solo campers prefer this setup over a full-width mattress.

Is the Havnby Solo as comfortable as the full-size Autolevel?

The Solo uses the same 6.7-inch high-resilience foam and zero-tilt design as the full-size Autolevel. The only difference is width - you get the same comfort level in a narrower profile.

How does the built-in pump work?

The Solo features a built-in 4000Pa dual pump that inflates the mattress in approximately 50 seconds. It runs off your Tesla's 12V outlet or can be charged via USB-C. The same pump deflates the mattress for easy packing.

What's the weight capacity of the Havnby Solo?

The Havnby Autolevel Solo supports up to 660 lbs (300kg), the same as the full-size version. This is more than adequate for virtually any single sleeper plus gear.

Is 32 inches wide enough to sleep comfortably?

For most adults, yes. 32 inches is comparable to a standard sleeping bag width. If you're a larger person or tend to move around a lot while sleeping, you might prefer the full-size Autolevel (52" wide). But for average-sized sleepers, 32 inches provides comfortable rest.

Does the Solo work with the 2025 Model Y Juniper?

Yes, Havnby offers versions for both the original Model Y (2020-2024) and the refreshed 2025+ Model Y (Juniper). Make sure to select the correct version when ordering.

Is the TPU material durable for pet claws?

Yes, the eco-safe TPU material is claw-resistant and pet-friendly. Many Solo owners specifically choose this mattress for camping with dogs. The waterproof surface also makes cleanup easy after muddy paw prints.

How long does the pump battery last?

The built-in pump holds enough charge for multiple inflation/deflation cycles. It recharges via USB-C from your Tesla's ports or any USB charger. For week-long trips, you may want to top up the charge every few days.

Can I use the Solo on either side of the car?

Yes, the Solo can be positioned on either the driver or passenger side. Most users place it on the passenger side since the driver's seat is often pushed further back, but either configuration works.

Does the discount code AWD work for the Solo?

Yes, code AWD provides 10% off all Havnby products including the Solo, Autolevel, and Foam versions. This saves approximately $24 on the Solo.
