Why the Complete Kit Matters

Most Tesla mattresses are just that - mattresses. You still need to buy a pump, find sheets that fit, and figure out storage. The TESMAT Luxe eliminates this guesswork by including everything you need:

The Mattress

The TESMAT Luxe uses a patent-pending hybrid design: a dual foam support layer paired with a 3D grid air base layer that contours to the Model Y's floor. This creates the flattest possible sleeping surface despite the car's uneven cargo area. The construction addresses complaints about the original TESMAT being too thin for side sleepers.

USB-C Rechargeable Pump

The included wireless pump inflates the mattress in under a minute and recharges via USB-C - the same cable you use for your phone. No hunting for adapters or proprietary chargers. The pump is compact enough to store in your center console.

Fitted Sheet with Retention Strap

Finding sheets that fit a Tesla mattress is surprisingly difficult. TESMAT includes a custom-fitted sheet designed specifically for the Luxe's dimensions. The retention strap keeps it in place overnight - no waking up on bare mattress.

Waterproof Carrying Case

The case isn't just for organization - it's waterproof to protect your mattress during outdoor adventures. Muddy campsite? No problem. It also keeps everything together when stored in your garage.

TESMAT Luxe vs. Original TESMAT

The original TESMAT (now called TESMAT Solo) received mixed reviews for comfort - great for occasional use, but too thin for serious car campers. The Luxe addresses this with:

  • Thicker foam layer - Better support for side sleepers
  • Hybrid air+foam construction - More cushioning than air-only
  • Complete kit - All accessories included vs. mattress only
  • Higher price ($379 vs $139) - Reflects the upgrades

If you're a back sleeper or only camp occasionally, the TESMAT Solo at $139 might be enough. For everyone else, the Luxe is the better choice.

Setup Guide: Complete Kit in Action

The TESMAT Luxe's Z-fold design and complete kit make setup straightforward.

  1. Fold down the rear seats - Pull the release levers to fold the Model Y's 60/40 split seats flat.
  2. Unpack the kit - Remove the mattress, pump, and fitted sheet from the waterproof carrying case.
  3. Unfold the Z-folded mattress - The three sections feature magnetic clasps that make positioning easy. The 3D grid air base will contour to your Model Y's floor.
  4. Connect the USB-C pump and inflate - The included rechargeable pump inflates the hybrid air+foam mattress in under a minute. Charge from your Tesla's USB ports if needed.
  5. Add the fitted sheet - Stretch the included custom sheet over the mattress for a comfortable, bed-like feel.
  6. Enable Camp Mode - Turn on Tesla's Camp Mode for climate control throughout the night.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a TESMAT discount code?

Unfortunately, TESMAT doesn't currently offer affiliate discount codes. The $379 price is the standard retail price. If value is your priority, consider the Havnby Autolevel with code AWD for 10% off.

Is the Luxe worth the upgrade from the original TESMAT?

If you're a side sleeper or plan to camp frequently, yes. The original TESMAT (now called Solo) received complaints about being too thin. The Luxe's hybrid construction addresses this with better foam support.

Can I use the TESMAT Luxe in both Model Y and Model 3?

The Model Y version is designed for the Model Y's larger cargo area. TESMAT makes a separate Model 3 version sized for the smaller interior. However, users report the Model 3 version works acceptably in a Model Y if needed.

Why does Electrek recommend TESMAT?

Electrek's endorsement cites the precise fit, complete kit, and the fact that it's made by Tesla owners who understand the car. They note "there are many decent options for Tesla mattresses, but none we found to be as good as the Tesmat."

How long does inflation take?

With the included USB-C pump, inflation takes under a minute. The pump is wireless and rechargeable, so you can inflate anywhere without needing the car's power.

Can the carrying case really double as a cooler?

Yes, the waterproof carrying case is insulated and can be used to keep drinks cold during your camping trip. It's a clever dual-purpose design that maximizes the value of the kit.

Is there a factory smell when new?

Some users report a slight initial smell that dissipates after airing out for a day. This is normal for foam products and not harmful. Leave it inflated in a ventilated area before your first trip if you're sensitive to new product odors.

How does TESMAT respond to user feedback?

TESMAT famously created their 2nd generation mattress (the Luxe) based on feedback from Reddit users who found the original too thin. This responsiveness to the community is one reason for their strong reputation.

Does the mattress work with Camp Mode?

Absolutely. The TESMAT Luxe is designed to work perfectly with Tesla's Camp Mode. The mattress doesn't block any vents, and you can maintain comfortable temperatures all night using your Tesla's climate control.

How does it compare to hotel savings?

At $379, you'd break even after about 3-4 nights of avoided motel costs. Users report the comfort is "better than motel comfortable" - so you're not just saving money, you're actually sleeping better than budget accommodation.

Is the built-in pillow any good?

The integrated pillow is functional but on the flatter side. If you prefer a plush pillow, consider bringing your own or purchasing Snuuzu's pillow bundle. Most users find it adequate for camping purposes.
