Setup Guide: From Trunk to Bed in 80 Seconds

The Havnby Autolevel's built-in 6000Pa pump makes setup incredibly fast.

  1. Fold down the rear seats - Pull the release levers to fold the Model Y's 60/40 split seats flat.
  2. Cover the seat latches - Place a towel or blanket over the exposed metal latches to avoid discomfort while sleeping.
  3. Remove the mattress from its carrying case - The compact storage case keeps everything organized.
  4. Position and press inflate - Place the mattress in the cargo area and press the built-in pump button. The 6000Pa pump inflates to full thickness in 80 seconds.
  5. Adjust firmness - Release air for a softer feel, or leave fully inflated for maximum support. The zero-tilt design automatically creates a level surface.
  6. Enable Camp Mode - Turn on Tesla's Camp Mode for climate control throughout the night.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Havnby Autolevel good for side sleepers?

Yes. The 7.5-inch foam provides excellent support for side sleepers. Multiple reviewers weighing 100+ kg report sleeping comfortably on their side without hip or shoulder pressure. You can also adjust firmness by releasing air to find your perfect support level.

How does the built-in pump work?

Simply press the power button to inflate or deflate. The 6000Pa pump inflates the mattress in about 80 seconds. It's rechargeable and doesn't require your Tesla's power - you can set up while the car is off.

Is it really waterproof?

Yes, the eco-safe TPU material is fully waterproof. This makes it ideal for camping with kids (spills happen) or dogs (the material is also claw-resistant). Wipe clean with a damp cloth.

Does it work with the 2025 Model Y Juniper?

Yes, Havnby offers versions for both the original Model Y (2020-2024) and the refreshed 2025+ Model Y (Juniper). Select the correct version when ordering.

What's included in the box?

The Havnby Autolevel comes with the mattress (built-in pump), storage bag, USB-C charging cable, and two eye masks. Some versions also include a fitted sheet.

Does the discount code AWD still work?

Yes, code AWD is currently active for 10% off at havnby.com. This saves you about $36 on the Autolevel.

Is the TPU material safe?

Yes, Havnby uses baby-safe TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) that's non-toxic and odorless. Unlike cheap PVC air mattresses that off-gas chemical smells, the Havnby has no noticeable odor out of the box. It won't harden or crack under extreme sun exposure.

How does the auto-level design work?

The mattress has a 3.2-inch slope compensation design built into the foam structure. When you fold down your Tesla's rear seats, they create an incline of roughly 7 degrees. The Havnby's asymmetrical foam filling is thicker where needed to counteract this slope, creating a level sleeping surface.

Does it lose air overnight?

Users consistently report the mattress maintains pressure throughout the night. The TPU material and sealed seams prevent air loss. If you notice slight softening overnight (normal for air mattresses), simply top up for 10-15 seconds in the morning.

Can I adjust the firmness?

Yes. After inflation, press the release valve to let out air for a softer feel. The 6000Pa pump can also add more air if you want it firmer. Most users find the default inflation level comfortable, but adjustability is there if needed.

What's the weight capacity?

The Havnby Autolevel supports up to 660 lbs (300 kg) combined weight. Users weighing 112+ kg have reported sleeping comfortably without bottoming out. The high-resilience foam core prevents the "hammock" effect that cheap air mattresses have.

How long does the pump battery last?

A full charge provides multiple inflate/deflate cycles - enough for a week-long camping trip. The pump recharges via USB-C, so you can top up from your Tesla's USB ports or a portable battery pack during the day.
