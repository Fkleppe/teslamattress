Setup Guide: Hotel Comfort in 60 Seconds

The Havnby Foam features one of the fastest setup times of any Tesla mattress thanks to its integrated pump.

  1. Fold down the rear seats - Pull the release levers to fold the Model Y's 60/40 split seats or Model 3's rear seats flat.
  2. Remove the mattress from its storage bag - At 22 lbs (10 kg), it's manageable but not ultralight.
  3. Position and unfold - Place the mattress in the cargo area with the pump toward the back.
  4. Press the inflate button - The integrated 6000Pa pump inflates the mattress in approximately 60 seconds. The upgraded durable pump motor is designed for extended use cycles.
  5. Adjust firmness - Add more air for firmer support, or release some air for a softer feel. Everyone's preference is different.
  6. Enable Camp Mode - Turn on Tesla's Camp Mode for climate control throughout the night.

Packing Up

Deflation takes about 50 seconds with the pump. The mattress compresses into its storage bag and fits in the Model Y frunk or Model 3 sub-trunk. The TPU waterproof material wipes clean easily before storage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Havnby Foam work in both Model Y and Model 3?

Yes, the Havnby Foam is designed to fit both Tesla Model Y and Model 3. The mattress dimensions and shape accommodate both vehicles' interior layouts, making it a versatile choice for households with multiple Tesla models.

How thick is the Havnby Foam compared to the Autolevel?

The Havnby Foam is 4.5 inches thick, compared to the Autolevel's 7.5 inches. While thinner, the Foam still provides decent comfort for occasional camping and is more compact for storage.

Is the Havnby Foam comfortable for heavier sleepers?

The Havnby Foam supports up to 660 lbs (300kg), but heavier sleepers may feel the car floor more due to the thinner 4.5-inch foam. For sleepers over 200 lbs who camp frequently, we recommend the thicker Havnby Autolevel (7.5 inches).

What's included with the Havnby Foam mattress?

The Havnby Foam comes with the mattress, a USB-C rechargeable pump for inflation/deflation, a storage bag, and 2 eye masks. The 5-year warranty is also included.

Is the Havnby Foam waterproof?

Yes, the Havnby Foam uses eco-safe TPU material that's fully waterproof and pet-friendly. It's easy to wipe clean after camping trips and resistant to muddy paws.

How fast does the Havnby Foam inflate?

The included USB-C pump inflates the mattress in approximately 60 seconds. The same pump deflates for easy packing. It recharges quickly from your Tesla's USB ports.

Does the Havnby Foam have the auto-level feature?

The Foam version has basic slope compensation, but the premium auto-leveling foam filler design is exclusive to the Havnby Autolevel model. If level sleeping is critical for you, upgrade to the Autolevel for $100 more.

Can two adults sleep comfortably on the Havnby Foam?

Yes, the 76" x 52" dimensions accommodate two adults. However, with 4.5 inches of foam, couples may prefer the thicker 7.5-inch Autolevel for added comfort, especially for side sleepers.

Does the AWD discount code work for the Foam?

Yes, code AWD provides 10% off all Havnby products including the Foam, Autolevel, and Solo versions. This saves approximately $26 on the Foam mattress.

The Bottom Line

The Havnby Foam is a practical choice for Tesla owners who value versatility over maximum comfort. Its ability to fit both Model Y and Model 3 makes it unique in the market, and Havnby's build quality and 5-year warranty provide confidence in the purchase.

At $259, it sits comfortably between budget options and high-end mattresses. The 4.5-inch foam won't feel like a real bed, but it's absolutely adequate for occasional camping trips. If you own or plan to own both a Model Y and Model 3, this is the only mattress that serves both.

For frequent campers who prioritize comfort, step up to the Havnby Autolevel with its thicker 7.5-inch foam. But for occasional use with the flexibility of dual-vehicle compatibility, the Foam delivers solid value.
