The 4-Layer Foam System

Snuuzu was founded in 2021 by Omar Tegel (one of the first Tesla Model S owners in the Netherlands, back in 2013), his son Per, and colleague Djai Bereiter. After 35+ years in product development, Omar was frustrated that no mattress met his standards for Tesla camping. Their three years of R&D resulted in a patent-pending 4-layer system:

Layer 1: Lyocell Cover

The top layer uses Lyocell (also known as Tencel), a fabric made from eucalyptus that's naturally temperature-regulating and moisture-wicking. This keeps you comfortable whether it's hot or cold outside.

Layer 2: Memory Foam

High-density memory foam that contours to your body and provides pressure relief. This is the same quality foam you'd find in higher-end home mattresses.

Layer 3: Asymmetrical Air Chambers

Snuuzu's patent-pending air chamber design creates a perfectly flat surface that compensates for the Model 3's folded seat angles. This is the secret to why it feels so much better than air mattresses.

Layer 4: Waterproof Base

A durable, waterproof base layer protects the mattress from moisture and dirt. Essential for camping conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you sleep comfortably in a Tesla Model 3?

Yes, with the right mattress. The Model 3 is smaller than the Model Y, but with a properly designed mattress like the Snuuzu Model 3, people up to 6 feet tall can sleep comfortably. The Model 3's flat folded seats and Camp Mode climate control make it viable for car camping.

Is the Snuuzu Model 3 the same quality as the Model Y version?

Yes, the Snuuzu Model 3 uses the exact same 4-layer construction: Lyocell top cover, memory foam layer, patent-pending asymmetrical air chambers, and waterproof base. The only difference is the dimensions, which are custom-cut for the Model 3's interior.

What is the maximum height for sleeping in a Tesla Model 3?

The Tesla Model 3 offers approximately 5'10" (178cm) of length when seats are folded. People up to 6 feet can sleep comfortably at an angle, though taller individuals may find the Model Y more suitable for extended camping trips.

Does the Snuuzu Model 3 require a pump?

The Snuuzu has an integrated rechargeable pump built into the mattress. Simply unfold it, press the inflate button, and the mattress inflates automatically. No external pump needed - ready for sleep in under 2 minutes.

Is the Model 3 mattress thinner than the Model Y version?

Yes, slightly. The Model 3 version is 18 cm (7.1") thick compared to the Model Y's 20 cm (7.9"). This is optimized for the Model 3's lower ceiling height, ensuring you have adequate headroom when sitting up. The comfort level is identical - it uses the exact same 4-layer construction.

Can two people sleep on the Model 3 Snuuzu?

Technically yes, but it's tight. The Model 3's interior is narrower than the Model Y. Two adults can fit, but it's more comfortable for solo sleepers or a couple where at least one person is average-sized. For couples who camp frequently, we recommend considering a Model Y.

Does the Snuuzu lose air overnight?

No. The TPU base and sealed air chambers maintain pressure throughout the night. Users consistently report waking up on a firm surface without needing to top up air. The mattress is designed for multi-night trips.

Does the Snuuzu work with the 2024 Model 3 Highland?

Yes, the Snuuzu Model 3 fits all Model 3 years including the 2024 Highland refresh. The interior cargo dimensions haven't changed significantly with the refresh.

What makes Lyocell special?

Lyocell (also called Tencel) is derived from sustainably-harvested eucalyptus wood pulp. It's naturally breathable, 30% more moisture-wicking than cotton, hypoallergenic, and silky-smooth to touch. Unlike PVC or polyester on budget mattresses, Lyocell doesn't trap heat or cause sweating.
