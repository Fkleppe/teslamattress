Setup Guide: From Trunk to Bed

The TESMAT Luxe's Z-fold design makes setup straightforward, even in the Model 3's more compact interior.

  1. Fold down the rear seats - Pull the release levers to fold the Model 3's seats flat. Cover any exposed metal latches with a towel if desired.
  2. Remove the kit from its waterproof case - The carrying case keeps everything organized and protected.
  3. Unfold the Z-folded mattress - The three sections have magnetic clasps that make positioning easy.
  4. Connect the USB-C pump and inflate - The air base inflates in under a minute. Charge the pump from your Tesla's USB ports.
  5. Add the fitted sheet - Stretch the included sheet over the mattress for a comfortable sleeping surface.
  6. Enable Camp Mode - Turn on Tesla's Camp Mode for climate control. Model 3 handles overnight climate well.

Model 3 Camping Tips

  • The Model 3's smaller interior means less air volume to heat/cool - your battery will last longer in Camp Mode
  • Park with the trunk facing away from bright lights for better privacy
  • The carrying case doubles as storage for your pillow and personal items while camping
  • Consider a window shade kit - the Model 3's glass roof lets in more light than the Model Y

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the TESMAT Luxe Model 3 the same quality as the Model Y version?

Yes, the TESMAT Luxe Model 3 uses the same patent-pending hybrid air+foam construction as the Model Y version. The only difference is the dimensions, which are custom-fitted for the Model 3's interior.

What's included in the TESMAT Luxe Model 3 kit?

The complete kit includes the Luxe mattress, a USB-C rechargeable pump, a custom fitted sheet, and a waterproof carrying case. Everything you need to start camping in your Model 3.

How does the TESMAT Luxe compare to the Snuuzu Model 3?

The TESMAT Luxe ($379) is more affordable than the Snuuzu Model 3 ($899) and includes more accessories. However, the Snuuzu offers superior comfort with its 4-layer foam system. Choose TESMAT for value and complete kit, Snuuzu for maximum comfort.

Can tall people sleep comfortably in a Tesla Model 3?

The Model 3 is more compact than the Model Y for sleeping. People up to about 5'10" (178cm) will be most comfortable. Taller individuals can fit at an angle, but extended trips may be less comfortable than in a Model Y.

Does the TESMAT Luxe Model 3 work with Camp Mode?

Yes, the mattress is designed to work perfectly with Tesla's Camp Mode. It doesn't block any vents, allowing you to maintain comfortable temperatures all night using your Tesla's climate control.

Does the Luxe Model 3 fit the 2024 Highland refresh?

Yes, the TESMAT Luxe Model 3 is compatible with all Model 3 years, including the 2024 Highland refresh. The cargo area dimensions are consistent across generations.

Can the carrying case really work as a cooler?

Yes, the waterproof carrying case is insulated and can keep drinks cold during your camping trip. It's a thoughtful dual-purpose design that adds value to the kit.

How comfortable is the TESMAT Luxe for couples?

Two adults can sleep on the Luxe Model 3, but expect cozy quarters. The Model 3's interior is narrower than the Model Y. For couples who camp regularly, consider if a Model Y might better suit your needs.

How does the USB-C pump work?

The included pump is wireless and rechargeable via USB-C. It inflates the mattress in under a minute and is compact enough to store in your center console. No need to find 12V adapters - just charge it like your phone.
