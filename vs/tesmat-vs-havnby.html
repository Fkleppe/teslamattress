Two popular options at nearly identical prices. Which offers better value at $350-380?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TESMAT or Havnby better?

Havnby Autolevel scores slightly higher (8.6 vs 8.5) at a slightly lower price ($360 vs $379). However, TESMAT includes a complete kit while Havnby is just the mattress. Both are excellent mid-range options.

Which is thicker?

Havnby Autolevel is significantly thicker at 19cm (7.5") compared to TESMAT Luxe at approximately 11cm (4.5"). This makes Havnby more comfortable for most sleepers, especially side sleepers.

Which has a better warranty?

Havnby offers a 5-year warranty, which is one of the best in the industry. TESMAT offers a standard warranty.

Havnby has code AWD for 10% off ($323.99 final price). We don't currently have a TESMAT discount code.
