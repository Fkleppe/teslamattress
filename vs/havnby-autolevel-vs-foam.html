Foam wins on simplicity and value at $259 with no pump needed and compatibility with both Model Y and Model 3. Frequently Asked Questions

Should I get Havnby Autolevel or Foam?

Get the Autolevel ($360) if you want better comfort with its 19cm thickness and self-inflating technology. Get the Foam ($259) if you're on a tighter budget and want a simpler, no-pump solution that works for both Model Y and Model 3.

What's the main difference?

Autolevel uses air-foam hybrid technology with a built-in pump (19cm thick). Foam is pure foam with no pump needed (11.4cm thick). Autolevel is Model Y only; Foam fits both Model Y and Model 3.

Is the Autolevel worth $100 more?

If you camp frequently and prioritize comfort, yes. The extra thickness makes a noticeable difference in sleep quality. For occasional camping, the Foam is sufficient.

Which is easier to set up?

Foam is simpler - just unroll and it's ready. Autolevel requires pressing a button and waiting 2-3 minutes for inflation, though this is still very easy.
