{{t.methodology.h2_2}}

{{t.methodology.p_2}}

  1. Purchase: We buy at retail price. If a brand offers a review unit, we still buy one ourselves for comparison.
  2. Initial testing: 5+ nights of actual sleep, in different temperatures and locations. We note comfort, setup time, and any issues.
  3. Long-term use: Reviews are updated after 3 months. Some mattresses that seem great initially develop problems over time.
  4. Comparison: When possible, we test multiple mattresses on the same trip to compare directly.

{{t.methodology.h2_3}}

{{t.methodology.p_3}}

  • Comfort (30%) - How well did we actually sleep?
  • Build Quality (25%) - Materials, durability, construction
  • Fit & Finish (20%) - How well does it fit the Tesla?
  • Ease of Setup (15%) - Can you set it up tired at midnight?
  • Value (10%) - Is the price justified by performance?

{{t.methodology.p_4}}

{{t.methodology.h2_4}}

  • Accept payment for reviews
  • Let manufacturers approve content before publishing
  • Remove negative reviews when asked
  • Test mattresses for just one night

{{t.methodology.p_8}}

  • Climate: Testing conducted primarily in Scandinavian conditions (cool to temperate). Performance may vary in extreme heat or cold.
  • Vehicle models: Limited to Tesla Model Y and Model 3. We haven't tested in Model S, Model X, or Cybertruck.
  • Sample size: One vehicle per model. Individual interior variations may exist between vehicles.
  • Durability: Long-term durability assessed to 6 months maximum. Multi-year durability is unknown.
  • Tester profile: Primary tester is average build. Very heavy or very light sleepers may have different experiences.
  • Subjective comfort: Comfort is inherently subjective. Our ratings reflect one family's experience.
