Fit check Why it matters How to verify it
Usable length Changes with front-seat position and mattress shape Measure your normal driving position, then compare the product diagram
Narrowest width Wheel wells and interior trim limit a rectangular pad Measure the narrowest intended sleeping area, not the widest cargo point
Remaining headroom Deeper mattresses reduce sitting and turning clearance Measure from the inflated top surface to the roof or glass
Seat layout Five-, six- and seven-seat configurations can create different transitions Fold the actual seats and inspect every hinge and height change before ordering

Because seat layouts vary, confirm the exact Model X configuration with Snuuzu before ordering.
