{{t.review_snuuzu_model_3.p_16}}

  1. Fold down the rear seats - Pull the release levers to fold the Model 3's seats flat. Cover any exposed metal latches with a towel for comfort.
  2. Remove the Snuuzu from storage - The mattress comes with a carrying bag for easy transport and storage.
  3. Position with pillow end toward front - The asymmetrical air chambers are designed to create a level surface despite the Model 3's seat angle.
  4. Charge and activate the pump - Connect to USB-C power if needed, then press to inflate. The integrated pump fills all four layers in about 2 minutes.
  5. Adjust firmness - The patent-pending air chambers allow you to fine-tune firmness by adding or releasing air.
  6. Enable Camp Mode - Turn on Tesla's Camp Mode for climate control. The Model 3's smaller interior heats/cools efficiently.
