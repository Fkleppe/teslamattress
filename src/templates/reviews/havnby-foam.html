  1. Fold down the rear seats - Pull the release levers to fold the Model Y's 60/40 split seats or Model 3's rear seats flat.
  2. Remove the mattress from its storage bag - At 22 lbs (10 kg), it's manageable but not ultralight.
  3. Position and unfold - Place the mattress in the cargo area with the pump toward the back.
  4. Press the inflate button - The integrated 6000Pa pump inflates the mattress in approximately 60 seconds. The upgraded durable pump motor is designed for extended use cycles.
  5. Adjust firmness - Add more air for firmer support, or release some air for a softer feel. Everyone's preference is different.
  6. Enable Camp Mode - Turn on Tesla's Camp Mode for climate control throughout the night.
