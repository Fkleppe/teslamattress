{{t.review_tesery_novapads.p_15}}

  1. Fold down the rear seats - Pull the release levers to fold the Model Y's 60/40 split seats flat.
  2. Remove the NovaPads from storage - When deflated, it's compact enough to fit in a trunk corner or under the cargo cover.
  3. Position and unfold - Place the mattress in the cargo area with the pump connector toward the back of the car.
  4. Connect to 12V outlet and inflate - Plug into your Tesla's 12V outlet and press inflate. The built-in pump fills the mattress in about 50 seconds.
  5. Adjust firmness - Add more air for firmer support, or release some for a softer feel. The reinforced air chambers create a level sleeping surface automatically.
  6. Enable Camp Mode - Turn on Tesla's Camp Mode for climate control throughout the night.
